Viking Repair Services

We repair Viking Professional ranges, built-in refrigerators, wine columns, and ice machines throughout Dallas-Fort Worth. Our in-home diagnostic visit is $90 and applied toward the repair, and we can repair every kind of Viking appliance from 48-inch ranges to undercounter refrigeration.

  • Precision calibration for dual-fuel ranges and induction cooktops
  • Compressor, evaporator, and control board replacement for Viking fridges
  • Wine and beverage column leak repair, gasket swaps, and lighting fixes

Typical problems

  • Refrigerator warm spots or freezer burn from faulty dampers
  • Water or refrigerant leaks inside column coolers
  • Ovens not holding temperature or cycling endlessly
  • Spark modules, igniters, or fan motors failing mid-service
  • Noise from condenser fans or sealed-system vibration
  • Software updates required after Viking control replacements

We stock high-failure parts locally and document every fault code we encounter so the next visit is quicker. If you prefer eco-friendly refurbishments, we can offer to let your tech use used parts that have been tested and covered by our warranty.
