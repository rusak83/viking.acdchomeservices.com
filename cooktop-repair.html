How we repair Viking cooktops

Each visit starts with a full diagnostic of power, gas delivery, and electronics. We photograph findings, walk you through the repair plan, and only proceed after approval.

Common issues we solve

  • Burners spark but never ignite
  • Induction zones showing error codes
  • Flames too high/low or uneven simmer
  • Downdraft or ventilation not operating
  • Cracked glass or damaged grates
  • Control knobs melting or seizing
